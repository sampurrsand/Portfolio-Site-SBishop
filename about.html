About Me

My name is Samantha, and I’m a UI designer based in Texas. I graduated from Southern Methodist University with a degree in management science. I started my career in software asset management, where I was largely focused on automating, improving, and designing processes. After creating several PowerPoints for my team, I realized I wanted to pursue a creative field because I loved the challenge of arranging content in a way that enhances user experience. While researching careers where I could use both creative and technical skills, I stumbled upon UI design, and the rest is history.

I enjoy every phase of the UX/UI process, but I love engaging with users the most because I like helping people and meeting their needs. My favorite phases include user interviews, usability testing, and creating the visual language for my design. What I love most about being a UI designer with a development background is being able to create a visually pleasing product from the ground up. I also like being able to wear many different hats so that I can see multiple perspectives of a problem before solving it. When I'm not working on projects, you can find me hanging out with my family and pets, practicing modern calligraphy, or falling down Wikipedia rabbit holes.
